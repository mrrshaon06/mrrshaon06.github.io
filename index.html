Short Bio

Dr. Mohammad Razaur Shaon is a highly motivated and results-oriented transportation engineering academic with extensive experience in applying statistical and machine learning techniques, spatial data analytics to perform complex transportation data analyses and develop effective solutions. Led research initiatives in the domain of connected and autonomous vehicles (CAVs), utilizing Virtual Reality technology, freight analysis, and safety and mobility-related analysis. Proposed statistical and econometric models to incorporate driver behavior related factors into crash analytics. Led several Federal and state sponsored research projects. Proven ability to lead and collaborate on multi-disciplinary research projects, analyze complex datasets, and develop data-driven solutions to improve transportation safety and mobility.
